Our Core Values and Doctrine

We believe in the bodily death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ (1 Cor. 15:3-4)
We believe in the complete plan of salvation, including the necessity of baptism by immersion. (Mk. 16:16; Acts 2:38; Mt. 10:32; Acts 22:16; Col. 2:12; 1 Peter. 3:21)

We believe in the Bible as the inspired word of God and our only rule of faith and practice. (2 Tim. 3:15-16)

We believe in the Holy Trinity. (Jn. 16:7-15; Rom. 8:6-17; 1 Cor. 12:12-13; Phil. 2:5-11; Col. 2:9)

We believe that Jesus is the Christ, the Son of the living God, and our personal Savior. (Mt. 10:32; 16:16; Acts 8:36-37; Rom. 10:10)

We believe in the literal reality of both heaven and hell. (Mt. 5:12; Lk. 16:19-31; Rev. 20:15; 21: 1-8)

We believe in the priesthood of all believers.
